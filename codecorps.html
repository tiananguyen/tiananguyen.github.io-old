BGCSV Google Code Corps

Google Code Corps is an AmeriCorps VISTA program to fight poverty in America by introducing computer science to underserved youth. As a teacher assistant through this program, I help bring computer science education to youth in the community at Boys & Girls Club of Silicon Valley. Through leading a class of about 25 students through projects in Google CS First and Scratch Programming, I hope to create a welcoming environment full of creativity and inspiration.
