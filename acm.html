SCU Association for Computing Machinery

Serving as the Underclassmen Representative in Santa Clara University’s Association of Computing Machinery— one of the largest computer science organizations on campus— I actively seek to promote the growth of computer science at SCU through helping host workshops and tutorials on a variety of subjects in tech and helping run our annual hackathon for social justice, Hack For Humanity. Ultimately, ACM strives to develop a passionate community that brings out the innovative and creative side of individuals while collaborating with others that share the same passion in programming.
