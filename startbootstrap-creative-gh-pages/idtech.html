iD Tech

Serving as an instructor at West Valley College iD Tech Camp and Stanford University iD Tech Alexa, I taught Javascript, Adventures in Game Design, and Scratch Programming to students ranging from the ages 7 to 15. Throughout the weeklong course I focused on teaching the fundamental skills relative to each of the courses in order to construct a foundation on which these students could ignite their passions and unleash their creativity in the world of programming. I additionally made an initiative to help students realize the culmination of their week’s work by guiding their progress towards a final idea —some of which were centered around social justice issues —that was important to them.
